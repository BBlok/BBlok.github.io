Word Chain

How Many Words Can You Chain Together?

This is a game that started out as a team project for one of my computer science classes at Texas A&M University where I worked as a programmer, main designer and artist of the project. The objective of the game is to come up with a series of words where the next word starts with the same letter as the ending letter from the previous word.

I later decided to expand the game by adding more modalities to the game such as having the option to play against the computer by taking turns and being able to choose whether to play using words in either English, Spanish, or Polish.

Game Specs

  • Made with Unity game engine
  • Programmed in C#
  • All visual assets (except some fonts) are original
  • Playable from the web and as a standalone app for Android devices
  • First version completed: Spring 2016
