Welcome!

I am William Bracho Blok, a current student from SMU Guildhall pursuing a Master of Interactive Technology in Digital Game Development with a focus on game/level design. This website contains some of the games and mods that I have worked on over the years both as individual/side projects and team projects. Some of my skills in making games includes designing 3D levels/environmnets, making 2D games using the Unity game engine and programming it in C#, and making 2D artwork.
