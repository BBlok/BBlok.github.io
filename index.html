Welcome!

I am William Bracho Blok, a current student from SMU Guildhall pursuing a Master of Interactive Technology in Digital Game Development with a focus on game design. I graduated from Texas A&M University (class of '16) with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and a Minor in Art. This website contains some of the games and mods that I have worked on over the years both as individual/side projects and team projects. Some of my skills in making games includes designing 3D levels/environmnets, making 2D games using the Unity game engine and programming it in C#, and making 2D artwork, including sprite animations.


I am currently learning to make 3D levels using Unreal Engine 4 as well as learning to work with some of the basic components that make a game, such as static meshes, BSP brushes, materials, lighting, Unreal Engine blueprints, terrains, particle effects, and much more!
