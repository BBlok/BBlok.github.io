Lunch Rush!

This is a 2D side-scroller game (2 player game) made for the Chillennium Game Jam (Fall 2016) at Texas A&M University on a weekend in less than 48 hours! And our game received a Special Recognition in Programming!

In this jam, we had to make a game based on the definition of the word "foofaraw" - "a great fuss or disturbance about something very insignificant. " (Dictionary.com), and we came up with a game where two students race through their their school's lunchroom to get pizza . They race through the school hallways and do whatever it takes to win the race and become the king of the cafeteria!

We made the game using the Unity game engine, and I worked mainly in the design of the game and making the art assets. I also contributed in the character (sprite) animation.

Game Specs

  • 2-player only
  • Made with Unity engine
  • Programmed in C#
  • All art assets (except some fonts) are original
  • Playable from web (newest version: web adapted) with keyboard
